Section 01

The Short Version

Important

ÉclaireSkin provides AI-generated skincare guidance. It is a guide, not a diagnosis — you know your skin best. For personalised medical advice, a dermatologist is always worth consulting.

Everything ÉclaireSkin tells you — skin observations, ingredient analysis, routine suggestions — is generated by artificial intelligence, personalised to the profile you provide. It is educational and informational, not medical or clinical.

This disclaimer applies to all features of the Service: the skin profile quiz, photo analysis, ingredient checker, routine builder, tips and articles, and any premium features.

Section 02

Not Medical Advice

ÉclaireSkin is not a medical service. Nothing in the Service constitutes:

  • Medical advice
  • Clinical diagnosis of any skin condition, disease, or disorder
  • A recommendation to start, stop, or change any medical treatment
  • A substitute for consultation with a dermatologist, doctor, or other qualified health professional

If you have concerns about a skin condition — particularly anything painful, rapidly changing, or affecting your health — please see a qualified medical professional. Do not delay seeking medical care because of anything you read in this Service.

Section 03

AI-Generated Content

All guidance, observations, and recommendations in ÉclaireSkin are generated by artificial intelligence — specifically the Claude API provided by Anthropic. AI-generated content is clearly labelled throughout the Service.

AI does not know everything about your skin, your health history, your allergies, or your circumstances. It works from the information you provide and the limits of its training. It can be wrong.

AI-generated content:

  • May not be accurate, complete, or appropriate for your individual situation
  • Is based on general skincare knowledge — not a clinical assessment of you
  • Should be used as a starting point for your own research, not as a definitive answer
  • Does not account for drug interactions, medical conditions, or prescription treatments you may be using

We do not warrant that AI-generated content will be error-free or uninterrupted.

Section 04

Photo Analysis

When you upload a photo, ÉclaireSkin generates descriptive observations about your skin appearance — for example, "skin appears dehydrated" or "barrier may be compromised." These are observational descriptions based on visual appearance, not clinical assessments.

What photo analysis never does

  • Diagnose any skin condition
  • Identify medical disorders
  • Assess moles or lesions
  • Replace a dermatologist's evaluation

What photo analysis does

  • Describes visible skin appearance
  • Suggests relevant ingredient types
  • Personalises to your skin profile
  • Flags areas worth monitoring

Photo quality, lighting, skin tone, and camera settings all affect observations. Results are a guide only. If you notice any unusual skin changes — new moles, changing lesions, persistent redness or irritation — please see a doctor.

Section 05

Ingredient Guidance

The ingredient checker provides contextual observations about skincare ingredients personalised to your skin profile. It is designed to help you understand ingredient lists — not to provide a safety certification or clinical assessment.

Ingredient observations are informational only. They do not constitute a warranty that any ingredient is safe for your individual skin, health circumstances, or medical conditions.

Important limitations:

  • Ingredient analysis does not account for your full medical history or any conditions you have not disclosed
  • If you have known allergies or sensitivities, always check the full product label independently
  • Pregnancy and breastfeeding guidance is provided as general cosmetic information — always confirm with your midwife, GP, or obstetrician
  • If you have a skin condition being treated by a doctor, check ingredient changes with them first

Always patch test new products, regardless of what any ingredient analysis tool tells you.

Section 06

Product Recommendations

Where ÉclaireSkin suggests product lines, these suggestions:

  • Are based only on brands you have explicitly listed — we never suggest brands unprompted
  • Reference product lines (e.g. "the CeraVe hydrating range"), not specific product SKUs
  • Are not influenced by advertising, affiliate commissions, or brand relationships — ÉclaireSkin accepts none of these
  • Do not account for regional availability, current formulations, or individual product variants

Product formulations change. Always read the current label of any product before use, particularly if you have allergies or sensitivities.

Section 08

When to See a Professional

Please consult a dermatologist or doctor if you experience any of the following — ÉclaireSkin is not the right tool for these situations:

  • A mole, lesion, or skin mark that is new, changing, or unusual
  • Persistent, painful, or rapidly worsening skin conditions
  • Suspected allergic reactions or contact dermatitis
  • Any skin concern during pregnancy that you are unsure about
  • Skin conditions that have been diagnosed and are under medical management
  • Any concern that is affecting your health, wellbeing, or daily life
A good dermatologist is irreplaceable. ÉclaireSkin is designed to complement professional care — not replace it.
